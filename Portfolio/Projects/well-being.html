Ranges of well-being

Well being is synonymous to health. At the mention of well being or health, the first that would come to your mind is being sick or not, that is the presence or absence of a disease or ailment. But, well-being is the overall events happening in the life of an individual. Ranging from your physical well-being, emotional well-being, social well-being, economic well-being, life satisfaction etc.

Physical well-being includes the totality of the lifestyle that you go through to ensure you live in good health, which also ensures that you are physically fit to carry out activities. Physical well being does not necessarily mean the absence of sickness. Activities such as avoiding harmful substances such as alcohol, eating balanced diet and taking supplements when necessarily, resting and relaxing, having good sleep, regular exercise. It should not be surprising that the absence of this lifestyle can help you prevent illness, rather than the absence which might lead to its cure of illness.

In this world full of up and down, no one is hundred percent emotionally stable. But your thoughts and the positivity habit you inculcate goes a long way in you having a good state of emotional well-being. Emotional well-being is affected by the type of company you keep. You find yourself in so many places such as your home, office, cafeteria, neighbourhood, religious gatherings, clubs, parties and all sorts, the way you include yourself amongst these people, your role, the way you see them and how you relate with them is another state of well-being known as Social well-being!

The state of Economic well being is not only characterized by an individual. It may be observed by the totality of an organisation, community or even a country at large. It deals with the fulfilment and satisfaction of with your income, which tend to have influence on the influences finances and economic resources. The income is directly related to the economic well-being of the individual. Subjects with higher income are likely to be in a good state of economic well-being. But expenses and wants can alter this fact. In the sense that subjects ability to make adequate financial decisions and understand how to control their expenses speaks a lot about their economic well-being.

However, how much or how high a subject is does not determine his/her emotional well-being. Life satisfaction well-being does not necessarily mean you have everything you ever wanted or wished for. It is an expression of showing happiness every now and then, even at the expense of not having all that you wanted. Life satisfaction well-being has to be with taking and enjoying life the way it is.
